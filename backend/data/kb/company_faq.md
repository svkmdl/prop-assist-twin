## 2. Viewing Appointments & Selection Process
**How do I secure a viewing for a property?**
Due to high demand in the German market, we follow a structured approach:
* **Initial Inquiry:** Please use the contact form on platforms like ImmobilienScout24 or our website.
* **Qualification Call:** Our agents will conduct a brief telephone interview to verify your criteria (income-to-rent ratio, move-in date, household size).
* **Individual Viewings:** We prioritize individual or small-group viewings to ensure you have time to inspect the property thoroughly.
* **Self-Disclosure:** Prospective tenants must submit a *Mieterselbstauskunft* (Self-Disclosure Form), a current *SCHUFA* report, and proof of income for the last three months.

## 3. Buyer Preparation & Acquisition
**What should I have ready before searching for a property to buy?**
Buying property in Germany involves specific legal and financial hurdles. To be competitive, we recommend:
* **Financing Certificate (*Finanzierungsbestätigung*):** Sellers generally only accept offers from buyers who have a preliminary loan approval from a bank.
* **Target Profile:** Clearly define your budget including *Kaufnebenkosten* (Closing costs such as 6% Property Transfer Tax in Hesse, 1.5% Notary, and 3.57% Brokerage fee).
* **Identification:** Under the Money Laundering Act (*Geldwäschegesetz - GwG*), we are required to verify the identity of all serious prospects via a valid ID or passport.

## 4. Seller Preparation & Property Valuation
**What information do I need to provide for a professional valuation?**
To ensure an accurate *Verkehrswertermittlung* (Market Value Assessment), please prepare the following:
* **Core Documentation:** * Current Extract from the Land Register (*Grundbuchauszug*).
    * Living space calculation (*Wohnflächenberechnung*).
    * Site plan (*Lageplan*).
* **Technical Details:** Year of construction, record of modernizations (windows, roof, heating), and the mandatory Energy Certificate (*Energieausweis*).
* **For Condominiums (ETW):** The Declaration of Division (*Teilungserklärung*) and the last three sets of minutes from the Owners' Association meetings (*Eigentümerversammlung*).

## 5. Maintenance & Management (WEG)
**How does XYZ Immobilienverwaltung handle building maintenance?**
* **Routine Inspections:** Our facility managers conduct quarterly walk-throughs of all managed objects.
* **Service Providers:** We work with a vetted network of local craftsmen (*Handwerksmeister*) to ensure high-quality repairs.
* **Capital Reserves:** We advise Owners' Associations on the appropriate level of *Instandhaltungsrücklage* (Maintenance Reserve) to ensure long-term value retention and avoid sudden special assessments (*Sonderumlagen*).

## 6. Financials & Utility Billing
**When do I receive my annual statement?**
* **Utility Statements (*Nebenkostenabrechnung*):** Legally, these must be provided within 12 months of the end of the billing period. We aim to issue all statements by June of the following year.
* **Rent Increases:** Any adjustments to the net cold rent (*Kaltmiete*) are strictly based on the local rent index (*Mietspiegel*) or contractual indexation agreements (*Indexmiete*).
