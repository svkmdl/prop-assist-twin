# Service Areas: XYZ Immobilienverwaltung GmbH

XYZ Immobilienverwaltung GmbH provides a comprehensive suite of services designed to navigate the complexities of the German real estate market. Our expertise spans residential sales, asset management, and strategic consulting for both domestic and international clients.

---

## 1. Advanced Buyer Onboarding & Strategy
We move beyond simple listings to ensure our buyers are "transaction-ready" in a competitive environment.
* **Financial Profiling:** Assisting in the calculation of total acquisition costs, including the *Kaufnebenkosten* (typically 10-12% above the purchase price).
* **Criteria Optimization:** Helping buyers balance "Must-Haves" versus "Nice-to-Haves" based on current market availability in specific districts.
* **Verified Search Profile:** Creating a digital profile that grants priority access to "Off-Market" opportunities before they reach public portals.

## 2. Full-Scale Seller Onboarding & Marketing
Selling a property with XYZ Immobilien ensures maximum market reach and legal security.
* **Market Value Assessment (*Verkehrswertermittlung*):** A multi-factor analysis considering the *Sachwert* (material value) and *Vergleichswert* (comparative value).
* **Document Procurement Service:** We handle the retrieval of missing documents from the building authorities (*Bauamt*) and Land Registry (*Grundbuchamt*).
* **Staging & Presentation:** Professional photography, 3D virtual tours, and high-end exposé creation to highlight the property's unique selling points.

## 3. Comprehensive Viewing Preparation
We ensure that every viewing is productive and provides the data necessary for a final decision.
* **Technical Briefing:** Providing prospective buyers with a summary of the building's energy efficiency (based on the *Energieausweis*) and recent maintenance history.
* **Pre-Viewing Checklist:** A customized guide for buyers to evaluate the building substance, common areas, and potential renovation needs.
* **Virtual Pre-Screening:** Utilizing video walk-throughs to save time for international or out-of-city clients.

## 4. Financing-Related Preparation & Support
While we do not provide loans directly, we prepare the comprehensive documentation required by German lenders.
* **The "Bank Folder" (*Bankenmappe*):** We compile the complete set of property documents required for a mortgage application (floor plans, site maps, insurance certificates).
* **Equity Calculation:** Helping clients understand the impact of their down payment on the interest rate (LTV - Loan to Value).
* **KfW Subsidy Advisory:** Identifying eligibility for low-interest government loans for energy-efficient homes.

## 5. Next-Step Guidance & Process Navigation
The path from "Interested" to "Owner" is managed through our proprietary roadmap.
* **Notary Liaison:** Coordinating with the notary's office for the draft purchase agreement (*Kaufvertragsentwurf*) and explaining legal clauses.
* **Timeline Management:** Providing a clear schedule of milestones: from the reservation agreement to the *Auflassungsvormerkung* (Priority Notice) and final payment.
* **Closing Support:** Monitoring the *Fälligkeitsmitteilung* (Payment Due Notice) to ensure a smooth transfer of ownership.

## 6. Property & Asset Management (Hausverwaltung)
For those looking beyond the purchase, our management division ensures long-term value.
* **Tenant Management:** Handling all communication, from lease drafting to annual utility billing (*Nebenkostenabrechnung*).
* **Maintenance & Modernization:** Overseeing routine repairs and strategic upgrades to improve the property’s energy class and market value.
* **Owner Representation:** Professional representation at Homeowners' Association (WEG) meetings.
